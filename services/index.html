Air Conditioner Clean and Check

Nervous about turning on your air conditioner this season? Let us do it for you! Take advantage of our Clean & Check service now, and when that first heat wave hits this spring you will have complete confidence that your air conditioning system is working properly and efficiently.

Clean & Check service includes:

  • Clean the Condenser coil
  • Clean evaporator coil
  • Check Freon Levels
  • Oil Fan & Blower motors
  • Clean re-usable filters, electronic air cleaners or change disposable filter (customer must provide new filter)
  • Check thermostat
  • Verify proper & efficient operation of entire system air conditioning system
